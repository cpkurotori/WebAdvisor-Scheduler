Welcome to Cybersession/Late Spring (May 22 - June 16, 2017)
We want your learning experience at Ohlone to be positive and successful.  Please pay attention to the following dates and deadlines:

  • Last day to add a Cybersession course without an add authorization code is May 21  
  • Last day to add a Cybersession course with an add authorization code is May 26
  • Last day to drop a Cybersession course and be eligible for a refund is 10% of class meeting times
  • Last day to drop a Cybersession course without a W grade is May 26
  • Last day to drop a Cybersession course with a W grade is June 9
  • NOTE: Cybersession courses are included in the total unit load for Spring 2017 semester; students registering for more than 17.5 units for the spring (including the Cybersession units) must meet with a counselor to request an overload. For more information, please see Unit Overloads.
